THE CLUB

Competitive EBFL League

We play in the competitive EBFL league based in Reading. Every match is a chance to test our skills and push the team to new heights. We pride ourselves on teamwork, discipline, and representing Prospect FC with pride.

Professional Media Coverage

Games are Recorded Live and Pictures are also taken. Highlights and Photos are posted on our socials — Website, YouTube and Instagram.

Cup Competitions

In addition to League Games, Prospect also partakes in other competitive cup games. We play teams from other Divisions in and out of Reading.

Professional Training

We have two training sessions a week — Mondays and Thursdays at 7:00 PM, with structured, high-quality sessions at Prospect Park.

Club Community

It's not just about the pitch — it's about being part of the family. We take part in numerous events off the pitch, growing and bonding together as one.
