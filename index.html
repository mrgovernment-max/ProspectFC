ABOUT PROSPECT FC

We have two training sessions a week — Mondays and Thursdays at 7:00 PM, with structured, high-quality sessions at Prospect Park.

Games are Recorded Live and Pictures are also taking. Highlights and Photos are posted on our socials
-- website,Youtube and instagram --

In adddition to League Games , Prospect Partakes in other competiitve cup games

We play in the competitive EBFL league based in Reading. Every match is a chance to test our skills and push the team to new heights. We pride ourselves on teamwork, discipline, and representing Prospect FC with pride. Join us, watch us, or be part of the journey — the pitch is where it all happens

It’s not just about the pitch — it’s about being part of the family. We take part in numerous events off the pitch, growing and bonding together as one.
